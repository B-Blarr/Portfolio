About me

Hi, I’m Benjamin. I’m curious, I learn fast, and I enjoy writing code that not only works, but is also well-structured and maintainable. When I hit a roadblock, I stick with it until I fully understand the problem. At the moment, I’m particularly interested in the intersection of frontend development and AI.

I’m based in Schöningen near Braunschweig / Wolfsburg / Magdeburg, very open to remote work and comfortable with commutes when needed.Relocation isn’t currently planned.

I enjoy learning new technologies and can quickly get up to speed with new tools and frameworks. AI isn’t just a trend for me — I’m interested in integrating it into products in a practical, meaningful way.

I work in a structured way: understand the problem first, break it down, then implement a clean solution. I care about readability, performance, and a great user experience.
